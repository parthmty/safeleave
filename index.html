Safe Leave is a web app that calculates how many days you can safely leave classes without affecting your minimum attendance criteria per subject.

FAQs

Why do I need SafeLeave ?

SafeLeave helps students to manage their attendance effectively and avoid the stress of worrying about attendance requirements. It also ensures that students are not penalized for unavoidable absences.

Is SafeLeave free to use?

Yes, SafeLeave is completely free to use.

How does SafeLeave work?

Simply enter your minimum attendance criteria and the number of classes you have already attended, and SafeLeave will tell you how many more classes you can safely leave without failing.

Is SafeLeave accurate?

Yes, the calculations used in SafeLeave is completely accurate and verified by our team. Though, it is recommended to double check the result for utmost assurance.

What devices does SafeLeave work on?

SafeLeave is a web app, so it works on any device with a web browser, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

What is the minimum browser version required for SafeLeave?

SafeLeave is compatible with most modern web browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Is my attendance data safe with SafeLeave?

Yes, SafeLeave takes data privacy and security very seriously. We don't store attendance data or any other student's data on our servers.
